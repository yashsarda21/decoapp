Features

Welcome to Hill View Lawn, the ultimate destination for grand events and celebrations. Situated on a sprawling area of approximately 70000 square feet, our venue offers an expansive and spacious setting, perfect for accommodating a large gathering of around 1500-2000 guests.

  • Our Hill View Lawn, also known as the Helipad, provides an extraordinary backdrop for your special occasions. With its vast expanse, it offers endless possibilities for creating unforgettable experiences, whether you are hosting a wedding, a corporate extravaganza, or a community festival.

  • The sheer size of our Hill View Lawn ensures that your guests have ample room to move around comfortably and enjoy the event to the fullest. This expansive space allows for various event setups, including seating arrangements, entertainment stages, and dining areas, providing the flexibility to tailor the venue to your specific requirements.

  • With its scenic surroundings and panoramic views, our Hill View Lawn offers a picturesque ambiance that adds a touch of grandeur to any event. Whether it's an outdoor wedding ceremony under the open sky or a vibrant music festival,the natural beauty of the surroundings enhances the overall atmosphere and creates a truly magical experience for your guests.

  • Our dedicated team of event professionals will work closely with you to ensure that every detail is meticulously planned and executed. From coordinating logistics to arranging additional services such as lighting, sound systems, and stage setup, we strive to exceed your expectations and deliver a flawless event.

  • At Hill View Lawn, we understand the importance of providing a memorable experience for both you and your guests. Our goal is to create an atmosphere that leaves a lasting impression, one that is tailored to your vision and exceeds your expectations

Book Hill View Lawn for your next grand event and let us help you create cherished memories in a venue that offers vast space, breathtaking views, and unparalleled possibilities.
