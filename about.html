Our Story

At Jaju Decorators and Events it is all about crafting moments to make an occasion timeless. Founder and Director Mr.Sanjay Jaju with a zeal for creativity and production and a vision to redesign Indian weddings in a contemporary way founded the company to pursue his passion.

  • A confluence of creativity, creation and charisma
  • Known for its personalized, customized and innovative decorations

In the span of 26 years he has managed and done several wedding ceremonies with his trademark aplomb and has created a niche in the wedding industry. Be it managing an event or creating new decors, the panache and passion to execute the task flawlessly remains the same.
